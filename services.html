Services

One-time Inspection

A thorough, single-visit inspection designed to evaluate the safety and compliance of your scaffolding setup. Our experts will identify potential hazards, verify structural integrity, and ensure adherence to relevant safety standards. Perfect for projects requiring immediate validation before work begins or as a final safety check.

Weekly Inspections

Regular weekly inspections to maintain the safety, stability, and compliance of your scaffolding throughout your project’s duration. Our team systematically identifies wear, damage, or hazards, providing detailed reports and actionable recommendations to prevent accidents and delays.

Audits

Comprehensive and in-depth assessments of scaffolding systems, focusing on compliance with health and safety regulations, industry standards, and project requirements. Our audits include structural inspections, documentation reviews, and tailored advice to ensure complete peace of mind.

Anchor Testing

Precise pull testing to verify the strength, reliability, and load-bearing capacity of scaffold anchor points. Our tests ensure compliance with industry standards and help prevent anchor failures, protecting both your workforce and project timelines.

Consultancy

Expert scaffolding consultancy tailored to your project’s needs. From initial planning and risk assessment to ongoing technical advice, we provide guidance to optimize safety, efficiency, and compliance. Whether it’s design reviews or problem-solving on-site, we’re here to help every step of the way.
