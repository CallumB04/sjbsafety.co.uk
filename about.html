About SJB Safety Services

We are a well-established and reputable company based in London, specializing in comprehensive scaffolding inspection services across England and Wales. Our dedicated team of highly trained scaffold inspectors brings a wealth of experience and expertise, ensuring that our clients receive top-quality service that adheres to all relevant legal and safety regulations.

We conduct thorough inspections to not only assess the structural integrity of the scaffolding, but also focus on the safety measures in place, significantly reduce the likelihood of accidents on-site and mitigate potential fines from the Health and Safety Executive.

Our process is streamlined and highly responsive, allowing us to typically arrange a site visit within 24 hours of your request. At SJB Safety Services, we serve a wide range of sectors across the construction industry, catering to the specific requirements of both new build projects and ongoing maintenance or regeneration work. Our mission is to deliver dependable, comprehensive, and timely scaffolding inspections, ensuring safety and compliance remain a priority at every stage of your project.
