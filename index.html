Why choose SJB?

We pride ourselves on our meticulous approach to scaffolding inspections. Our inspectors deliver an extensive range of services that align with current regulatory frameworks, providing peace of mind for our clients. Our detailed inspection reports highlight compliance with key standards, including:

  • TG20:21 - Technical Guidance for Tube and Fitting Scaffolds, which provides essential information for the design and construction of safe scaffolding systems.
  • BS EN 12811 - British/European Standard for Tube and Fitting Scaffolds, ensuring that our practices meet industry benchmarks for safety and performance.
  • BS EN 12810 - British/European Standard for System Scaffolds, focusing on the safe assembly, use, and dismantling of system scaffolds, with reports delivered within 24 hours of the inspection.

Whether you require ongoing weekly inspections or a one-time comprehensive assessment, we are committed to helping your organization fully comply with the 'Work at Height Regulations 2005'. Our thorough inspections not only assess the structural integrity of the scaffolding but also focus on the safety measures in place, significantly reducing the likelihood of accidents on-site and mitigating potential fines from the Health and Safety Executive.

Our process is designed to be efficient and responsive; typically, we can schedule a site visit within 24 hours of your request. SJB Safety Services caters to a diverse range of sectors within the construction industry, addressing the unique needs of both new build projects and ongoing maintenance and regeneration efforts. Our goal is to provide reliable, thorough, and timely scaffolding inspections that prioritize safety and compliance at every stage of your project.
